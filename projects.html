Permission Granted

Permission Granted is built for videographers, vloggers, and anyone who regularly uses release forms. The purpose of Permission Granted is to allow people quick and easy access to their release and consent forms when filming people. Permission Granted will allow you to create, import, and edit release and consent forms.
